Brand Identity

Soulful Sevara

In this project, I crafted a fictional concert event dedicated to my favorite singer, Sevara Nazarkhan. Celebrated for her musical prowess, particularly in the Uzbek language, the project extends to the creation of a promotional poster for an imaginary U.S. tour, envisioning a spectacular comeback concert for the artist.

Bakers Cakers

The "Bakers Cakers" project revolves around a delightful Instagram business specializing in crafting homemade cakes for birthdays and small events. Enlisted to assist my friend, the unique charm of this venture lies in its logo, starring her adorable two cats. This whimsical touch adds a personal and playful flair to the brand, making "Bakers Cakers" a sweet standout in the world of delectable treats.
