Typography

Type Identity: Palatino Font

The Typography Project sought to link the release year of the Palatino font (1949) with a pivotal historical event from the same year. Exploring the Soviet repression of intellectuals and cultural suppression in Uzbekistan during that time, the project delves into personal reflections on the impact of such events. It highlights the consequences of cultural oppression, emphasizing the importance of preserving national ideals and reflecting on personal experiences that shaped my perspective on cultural identity, education, and the challenges of freedom of expression.

Cook Book of the Uzbek Cuisine

This project focuses on presenting the rich culinary heritage of Uzbekistan. One notable chapter, "The Main Dishes," showcases a selection of my favorite traditional dishes. By sharing the recipes and stories behind these culinary treasures, the project aims to celebrate the cultural significance of Uzbek cuisine. It invites readers to savor the flavors, textures, and historical narratives embedded in each dish, creating a connection between the art of cooking and the cultural identity of Uzbekistan.
