Photography

Frozen

The project "Frozen" delves into the realm of still life photography, capturing the exquisite beauty of frozen fruits, berries, and flowers encased in ice cubes. Through meticulous composition and lighting, this series transforms ordinary elements into mesmerizing visual narratives. Each photograph encapsulates the ephemeral moment when nature's vibrancy meets the arresting stillness of frozen forms.

Portraits: Black and White

This collection highlights the masterful dance between light and shadow, beautifully captured in compelling black and white portrait photography. Each image captures the essence of individuals, using the absence of color to emphasize the nuanced contrasts. This collection invites viewers to witness the expressive power of form and shadow, revealing a visual narrative that transcends the boundaries of color.
